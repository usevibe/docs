## Overview

The Vibe ecosystem consists of four primary entry points:

1. Vibe mobile app
2. Vibe website
3. Vibe partner app
4. Vibe smart glasses

   Each option is designed for a specific type of user and level of engagement.

## Getting Started with Vibe mobile app

The Spotlight mobile app is the fastest and most convenient way to access Spotlight.

<Frame>
  ![Untitleddesign](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
</Frame>

### Steps

1. Download the Vibe app from the App Store or Play Store.
2. Create your Vibe account using email, phone number or social login.
3. Set your location and preferences.
4. Browse available experiences near you or in any destination.
5. Book experiences directly through the app.
6. Access your bookings, tickets and recommendations in one place.

### Best for

- Everyday users
- Travelers
- Experience discovery
- Real-time booking

## Getting Started with the Vibe website

The Vibe website provides a larger, more detailed interface for planning and managing experiences.

### Steps

1. Visit the official [usevibe.net](http://usevibe.net) website.
2. Create or log into your Vibe account.
3. Search for destinations, experiences, or categories.
4. View detailed experience information.
5. Plan and book experiences.
6. Manage your bookings and account.

### Best for

- Desktop users
- Multi-tab browsing
- Easier comparison between multiple experiences
- Ideal for professional and operational use

## Getting Started with Vibe partner app

<Frame>
  ![Login(1)](/images/Login\(1\).png)
</Frame>

Vibe partners can list experiences and reach a global audience.

### Steps

1. Register as a partner on the Vibe partner platform.
2. Complete your business profile.
3. Submit your experience listings.
4. Add descriptions, pricing, images, and availability.
5. Publish your experiences.
6. Manage bookings and track performance.

### Benefits

- Access to Vibe's user base
- Increased visibility
- Booking management tools
- Analytics and insights

### Best for

- Experience providers
- Businesses

## Steps

1. Purchase Vibe AR glasses.
2. Connect the glasses to your Vibe account.
3. Pair with the Vibe mobile app.
4. Explore AR-enabled locations and experiences.
5. Access immersive overlays, navigation, and interactive content.

## Benefits

- Immersive experience layer
- Real-time contextual information
- Enhanced exploration

## Best for

- Early adopters
- Explorers
- Premium users
