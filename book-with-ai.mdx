## Vibe Platform

Get started your way, download the Vibe app on [iOS or Android](www.google.com), or book directly via the [website](google.com). Follow the steps below to book your first experience.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app or website">
    Browse experiences or use search to find what you’re looking for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select experience">
    Click on any experience to view details, timings, and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap “Book Now”">
    Choose your preferred slot or ticket option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your booking">
    Review details and proceed to checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay & access your ticket">
    Complete payment and your ticket will be added to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
