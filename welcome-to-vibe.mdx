### What's new? 

At Vibe what we are building isn't a recommendation engine with a better UI. Any player can bolt an LLM onto a search bar and call it AI. That's not what Vibe is.

Vibe agents don't suggest, they execute. They coordinate across multiple venues in a single booking, manage group bookings and build full itineraries that actually get confirmed end-to-end.

No existing marketplace platform does this. Not because they haven't thought of it, because it requires owning the entire transaction layer, not just the discovery layer.

That's the moat. We don't sit on top of the booking process. <kbd>We are the booking process.</kbd>
