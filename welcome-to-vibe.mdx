### What's new?

The question we get most: **why can't big players just do this?**

Because what we're building isn't a recommendation engine with a better UI. Any player can bolt an LLM onto a search bar and call it AI. That's not what Vibe is.

Vibe's agents don't suggest, they execute. They coordinate across multiple venues in a single booking, manage group bookings and build full itineraries that actually get confirmed end-to-end.

No existing marketplace platform does this. Not because they haven't thought of it, because it requires owning the entire transaction layer, not just the discovery layer.

**That's the moat. We don't sit on top of the booking process. We are the booking process.**
