## Products

The Spotlight ecosystem consists of a suite of integrated products designed to deliver a unified experience marketplace across mobile, web, and extended reality (XR). Each product plays a distinct role while operating seamlessly with the underlying Spotlight infrastructure.

  <Card icon="globe" href="https://nextjs.org" title="Spotlight Web App">
    Enables access to Spotlight’s planning and discovery capabilities through the browser.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="apartment" href="https://spotlightnow.in" title="Spotlight Partner App">
