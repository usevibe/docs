## Products

Vibe ecosystem consists of a suite of integrated products designed to deliver a unified experience marketplace across mobile, web, and extended reality (XR). Each product plays a distinct role while operating seamlessly with the underlying Spotlight infrastructure.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card icon="smartphone" href="https://expo.dev" title="Vibe app">
    The primary consumer interface for planning and booking lifestyle experiences on iOS and Android
  </Card>
  <Card icon="globe" href="https://nextjs.org" title="Vibe website">
    Enables access to Spotlight’s planning and discovery capabilities through the browser.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="briefcase-business" href="https://spotlightnow.in" title="Vibe partner">
    Manage listings, pricing, and availability in real time. Track performance, bookings, and customer insights in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="glasses" title="Vibe glasses">
    AI powered smart glass with built-in cameras, spatial audio and an immersive visual display.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="bitcoin" title="VibeCoin">
    VibeCoin is a blockchain-based token built on the [ERC-20](https://ethereum.org) standard, designed to power transactions, rewards and experiences within the Vibe ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="image" title="Vibe NFTs">
    Vibe NFTs are digital collectibles that users can earn on every visit to venues and trade freely with friends or on open marketplaces like [OpenSea.com](http://openSea.com)
  </Card>
</Columns>
